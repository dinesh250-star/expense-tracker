Uddhar book
Udhaar App helps you manage your money flow and be in complete control of your business.
It is complete accounting in itself.
It is much more simpler than double entry accounting methods like Tally.
No prior accounting knowledge is required to use this application.
1.Enter the amount you want to add on credit column.
2.Enter the amount you want to deduct on debit column.
3.The values entered should not be negative.
4.If you have null value you can enter zero but dont leave it empty.
5.After entering the values click on submit button
6.You can check the values by clicking on check balance.
